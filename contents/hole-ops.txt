== Hole Ops

Hole Ops can be one or more locations as specified in the Coordinates tab.
At least one location must be entered.

Hole Ops are done in this order:

. Spot
. Drill
. Ream
. Chamfer
. Tap
. Counterbore

If more than one Hole Op is enabled then Tool#'s must be used.
