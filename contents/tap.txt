== Tap

Tapping Ops require that M3/M4 work with your spindle and you have connected
spindle feedback as shown in the LinuxCNC manual.

. Select a thread form.
. Select a thread size.
. Click on Enable.
. Add a thread depth as a positive number.
. Add the tapping RPM.
. Optional add a Tool# if this is the only Op. If more than one Op the Tool#
  is mandatory.
